Ants on a Log

Description

Ants on a log is a timeless snack for all ages. the healthy celery stick topped with the savory peanut butter and the deliciously sweet raisins are a classic after school snack. I remember having these after school for many years

this simple recipe is sure to make your little ones jump for joy and maybe even be excited to help out in the kitchen on this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 celery sticks
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup raisins

Instructions

  1. Rinse off celery sticks and pat dry with paper towel. Cut each stick in half
  2. Spread peanut butter in the cavity of each celery stick
  3. Press raisins on top of peanut butter
